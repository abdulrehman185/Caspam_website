Welcome to CASPAM

Welcome to the CASPAM stands for the Centre for Advanced Studies in Pure and Applied Mathematics, a prominent department located at Bahauddin Zakariya University (BZU) in Multan, Pakistan. It is a premier institution dedicated to higher education, research, and development in various branches of mathematical sciences, aiming to connect mathematics with industry, society, and technology

Our Mission: CASPAM envisions to attain heights of excellence through academic teaching, learning and research so as to develop individuals, industry and society intellectually, professionally and ethically.
